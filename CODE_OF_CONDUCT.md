# Code of Conduct

FontARA should be a respectful and useful space for users, contributors, and
maintainers.

## Standards

Expected behavior:

- Be respectful and patient.
- Assume good intent, but accept correction when something goes wrong.
- Keep feedback specific, actionable, and focused on the work.
- Respect privacy and avoid sharing private information.
- Help keep issues and pull requests reproducible and on topic.

Unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Sexualized or violent language unrelated to the work.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated off-topic or disruptive comments after a maintainer asks you to stop.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove comments and contributions that do
not follow this code. Serious or repeated violations may result in temporary or
permanent limits on participation.

Security or abuse reports should be sent privately to the maintainer rather than
posted publicly.
